About Me

Hi, I'm Nafees Shaikh — a passionate Data Science graduate with a B.Sc. in Data Science and hands-on experience in Data Analysis, Data Annotation, and AI-driven solutions. Skilled in Python, SQL, Power BI, Tableau, Excel, and Machine Learning, I specialize in building interactive dashboards, automation tools, and intelligent applications that solve real-world business problems.

From developing a Smart Inventory Management System with demand forecasting to creating an AI-based Fingerprint & Signature Recognition System and Auto Data Cleaner Pro, I enjoy transforming complex data into meaningful insights and practical solutions.

Through industry simulations with Deloitte, Tata Group, and Quantium, along with professional experience in AI data annotation, I gained exposure to business intelligence, data quality, analytics, and operational reporting. My work earned 3rd Prize at a National-Level Model Making Competition for an AI-powered biometric authentication system built using Python and OpenCV.

Driven by curiosity and continuous learning, I'm always eager to explore emerging technologies, uncover valuable insights from data, and contribute to innovative, data-driven solutions that create real business impact.
